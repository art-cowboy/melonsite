Performative Artmaking

I use my trans* body-world to abstract and satirize gender tropes that manifest within Art world processes. I use art to extend, poeticize, and most importantly manipulate my trans* queer body - as my trans* queer body abstracts and manipulates the art simultaneously. I want to continue not knowing if trans* + art = trans* art. I am a post-passer, a post-dragger, a performative art-maker.
