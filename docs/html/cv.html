Artist Statement:


I use my trans* body-world as a starting place to abstract and satirize gendered tropes that manifest within the processes of art-making in the Art world. I use art to extend, poeticize, and most importantly manipulate my trans* queer body. I am a post-passer, a post-dragger, a performative art-maker.
